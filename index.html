Exploring our city. On foot.

×

In January 2023, we set out to experience Vancouver in a new way. Every Sunday, rain or shine, we'd plan and walk a ~12km route, weaving through neighborhoods, streets, and trails.

It's almost been two years and we're still at it! Why? Because we love getting outside and having multi-hour conversations while stumbling upon local gems like eateries, scenic spots, markets, and art displays.

Follow our journey, and maybe find inspiration to explore your own surroundings. Whether you’re a longtime resident or a visitor, these walks reveal a side of your city that’s off the beaten path!

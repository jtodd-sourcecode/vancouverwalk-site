Exploring our city. On foot.

×

In January 2023, we set out to experience Vancouver in a new way. Every Sunday, rain or shine, we plan and walk a 10+ km route that weaves through neighborhoods, streets, and trails.

It has been two years and we're still at it! Why? Because we love getting outside and having long conversations while stumbling upon local gems like eateries, scenic spots, and farmers markets.

Check out our journey, and maybe find inspiration to explore your own surroundings. Whether you’re a longtime resident or a visitor, these walks reveal a side of the city that’s off the beaten path!
